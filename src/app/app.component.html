"This Minecrafter Does Not Exist" is an experiment to see if we could recreate the GAN phenonomen known as "this person does not exist" in the context of the voxel sandbox game Minecraft. More importantly, we hope this website can introduce people to what GAN's are in a fun, interactive format. We hope you enjoy this as much as we did! We trained a GAN on over 600,000 Minecraft skins, and we provide all of the results in the gallery below. You can view the skins in 3d and download them for your personal use if you find any that look decent ;). If you are interested in how GAN's work, and how we applied GAN's to one of the best games on earth, checkout the learn section! We describe the resources used, our data wrangling, and how we managed to put them all together.
